Who We Are

21+ Years of Global Placements

Kannamani Consultants Private Limited is a government-licensed overseas recruitment and manpower consultancy headquartered in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Founded in 2005, we have spent over two decades connecting skilled and unskilled Indian workers with verified employers across Europe, the Gulf, and beyond.

We specialize in sectors including construction, warehouse operations, manufacturing, and skilled labor — managing the complete journey from candidate selection through to international job placement and ongoing support.

Our commitment is simple: transparent, ethical, and effective recruitment that opens real doors of opportunity for Indian workers while giving foreign employers access to reliable, trained manpower.
