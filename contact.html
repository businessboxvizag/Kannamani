FAQ

Common Questions

Is the initial consultation really free?

+

Yes — the first 30-minute discovery call is completely free with no obligation. It's a chance for us to understand your goals and see if we're a great fit for each other.

What is the minimum investment amount?

+

I work with clients across all asset levels. For dedicated portfolio management, a starting capital of ₹10 Lakhs is typical. However, consultations and business strategy services have no minimum.

Do you work with NRI clients?

+

Absolutely. I specialize in NRI financial planning including FEMA compliance, DTAA optimization, NRE/NRO account strategies, and cross-border wealth management for clients in UAE, UK and USA.

How are your fees structured?

+

Fees vary by service. Portfolio management is typically AUM-based (0.75–1.5% annually). Tax planning and business consulting are project-based or retainer. All fees are fully transparent with no hidden charges.
