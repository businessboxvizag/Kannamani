FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying for an overseas job through us.

Is the application really free? +
Yes. Submitting your application and our initial assessment are completely free. We maintain a transparent, upfront fee structure for documentation and processing — with no hidden charges at any stage.
Which countries are you recruiting for? +
We currently place workers across the Gulf — including UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia and Qatar — and Europe, including Serbia. Openings vary by season and sector, so reach out for the latest live positions.
Do I need prior experience to apply? +
Not always. While skilled roles require relevant trade experience, we also place general and unskilled workers in housekeeping, warehouse and helper positions — open to motivated candidates with no prior experience.
How long does the process take? +
It depends on the country, sector and your document readiness. Typically, candidates move from application to departure within 2–6 months. Our team keeps you informed at every step — from interview to visa to travel.
Is the recruitment government-compliant? +
Absolutely. All our recruitment activities strictly follow India's emigration regulations and bilateral labor agreements. We are a licensed consultancy and every placement is fully legal and documented.
